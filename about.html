Our Story

Founded in 2015, AutoMarket has grown from a small local dealership to one of the most trusted names in the used vehicle market. Our journey began with a simple mission: to make buying used cars and bikes transparent, reliable, and hassle-free.

Over the years, we've helped over 10,000 customers find their dream vehicles at the best prices. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred choice for vehicle buyers across the region.

We believe in complete transparency – every vehicle comes with a detailed history report, and we encourage test drives and independent inspections. Our team of certified mechanics ensures that every vehicle meets our high quality standards before listing.
